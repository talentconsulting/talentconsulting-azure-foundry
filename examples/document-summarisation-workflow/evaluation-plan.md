# Evaluation Plan: Document Summarisation Workflow

## Goals

- Summaries are faithful to the source document.
- No unsupported facts are introduced.
- Sensitive content warnings are applied correctly.
- Output follows the required structure.

## Acceptance Criteria

- 95 percent of summaries contain no material unsupported claims.
- 100 percent of restricted documents are blocked or escalated.
- 90 percent of outputs match the required structure.
