# Risk Assessment: Support Triage Agent

## Intended Use

The agent supports support-ticket routing. A human specialist remains responsible for final handling.

## Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Residual risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Misrouting a ticket | Medium | Medium | Human review and routing evaluation | Low |
| Missing a security escalation | Low | High | Dedicated critical-case tests and escalation rules | Medium |
| Exposure of sensitive ticket content | Low | High | Read-only tools and data handling policy | Medium |
