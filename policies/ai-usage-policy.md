# AI Usage Policy

## Approved Use

AI assets in this repository may be used only for documented business purposes approved by the asset owner.

## Prohibited Use

- Processing secrets or credentials unless explicitly approved.
- Processing regulated personal data without a documented legal basis and data owner approval.
- Making fully automated high-impact decisions without approved human oversight.
- Expanding tool permissions beyond the approved manifest.
- Deploying draft or unreviewed assets to production.

## Human Oversight

Human review is required when outputs affect customers, employees, financial decisions, legal positions, safety, compliance, or material business records.

## Escalation

Unexpected behavior, suspected data exposure, policy violations, or harmful outputs must be escalated to the asset owner and governance contact.
